Quick Start Guide

  1. Connect your VIRC hardware (Raspberry Pi Pico) to your computer
  2. Ensure the firmware is uploaded to the Pico
  3. Click "Connect to VIRC" to establish connection
  4. Click "Start Listening" to begin monitoring IR signals
  5. Point any IR remote at the VIRC receiver and press buttons
  6. Watch the IR codes appear in the log
